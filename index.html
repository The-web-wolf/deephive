Our Services

We focus on your business

We value every project by providing an interdisciplinary team that aligns with your needs with the necessary support you to make sure that your project attains success. Working together with our clients allows us to see beyond the code and grasp their business objectives.

01. Enquiry

You can get in touch with us either via email to hello@deephive.org or fill out our request an estimate form. Either way, we need to understand your vision at a high level so we can position ourselves to be as helpful as possible. It doesn’t matter if you’re not technical, that’s what we’re here for. Just focus on the business objectives you’d like to achieve and we’ll worry about the rest.

02. Initial discussion

A Director or Senior Manager will respond within one business day to set up a 15-30 minute meeting. We’re keen to learn more about your business goals and provide you with no obligation advice on how best to move your project forward. You’re welcome to follow up with any additional questions you may have after this initial discussion.

03. Cost and timescale estimates

99 out of 100 customers have two burning questions – how much will it cost, and how long will it take? While we can’t provide a fixed quote for cost and timescales at this early stage, we can often reference projects similar to yours that we have undertaken as a baseline to provide an estimate.

04. Requirements gathering

There are several ways we carefully inspect and define the technical side of your project, most depend on the scope and your preferred level of involvement. Our most popular approach, our Roadmap takes your project from high-level concepts and scribbled down ideas to development ready in one straightforward workshop.

05. Proposal

Following the assessment of your requirements, you can expect to receive a detailed plan that outlines: key milestones, costs, and timescales, risk mitigation, the tools and technologies that we use to build your solution

06. Flexible Development

Your project is now in the best position possible to succeed. We’ve identified and mitigated any risks, set down a detailed plan for how we will deliver and now the most exciting part can finally begin. You will be assigned a Project Manager who will build a communication plan customized for you and your team.

07. Free support and maintenance period

We provide free support for 3 months as part of all new software development builds plus a technical support team to provide service level agreement driven support and maintenance. Following this free period, we’ll provide you with a customized support and maintenance plan that meets your particular business needs.

08. Ongoing partnership

We build long-term relationships with our customers and work as their technology partners for several years in most cases. Our flexible solutions are designed to ensure we continue to meet your ever-changing needs and also help bring new opportunities for growth to your business.
